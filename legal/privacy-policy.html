Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Bro ("we", "us", or "the app") is a social application that lets friends communicate through preset expressions and send virtual currency called Bro Bucks (BB). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account we collect your email address, display name, and a profile image you optionally upload. If you sign in with Google or Facebook we receive only the basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) that those services share through their standard OAuth flow.

Profile Details. You may optionally add your workplace or school and one "interesting thing" about yourself. Workplace and school entries can be verified by peers.

Device Information. We generate an anonymous device fingerprint (a one-way hash of your device model, OS version, and a random salt) solely to enforce our one-account-per-person policy. We do not collect your device IMEI, advertising ID, or precise hardware serial numbers.

Location. The Bro-ximity feature uses your approximate location only while you are actively using that feature. Location data is never stored on our servers or shared with other users beyond the "nearby" indicator.

Transaction Data. We record Bro Bucks purchases and peer-to-peer transfers (bro-nations) to maintain your wallet balance and for platform integrity. BroCoin minting events are logged in an anonymized public ledger that does not reveal recipient identities.

Push Notification Tokens. If you enable notifications we store the Expo push token associated with your device so we can deliver bro request alerts, bro-nation receipts, and BroCoin mint notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app's features
  • Process in-app purchases and peer-to-peer Bro Bucks transfers
  • Enforce one-account-per-person limits and prevent abuse
  • Send push notifications you have opted into
  • Generate aggregated, anonymized platform statistics (total transactions, treasury reserve)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers. Information is shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • Other Users. Your display name, profile image, workplace/school, and "interesting thing" are visible to authenticated users. Your bro list is never visible to other users. BroCoin balances are private.
  • Service Providers. We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for authentication, database, and file storage. Data is processed under Google's data processing terms.
  • Legal Requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below. Upon deletion, profile data is removed within 30 days; anonymized ledger entries and aggregate statistics are retained.

5. Security

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, and Cloud Storage security rules to protect your data. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption. Profile images are limited to 5 MB and validated as image files before upload.

6. Children's Privacy

Bro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Choices

  • Profile. You can update or remove your profile image, workplace/school, and "interesting thing" at any time.
  • Notifications. You can disable push notifications through your device settings.
  • Account Deletion. You can request full account deletion by emailing us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. Continued use of Bro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

Questions or requests? Email us at privacy@broapp.com.
